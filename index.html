    As a recent graduate of the Coding Boot Camp at UNC Chapel Hill, I am looking forward to helping build and maintain some of today's best and most innovative technologies as a full stack web developer. I am constantly trying to hone my coding skills, and have many projects in mind in the near future, including a facial recognition song recommendation app!

    I find myself most interested in back end database technology. It's both rewarding and oddly satisfying to store and cleany retreive data from SQL and MongoDB databases using Express, and look forward to increasing my skills with back end technology!

    In my free time I enjoy playing sports, listening to music (the louder the better), and enjoying quality time with my friends and family. I hope to collaborate with my recently graduated classmates and other coders in the near future to work on pet-projects that I have in mind. It's a good way to practice my skills and do something I and my co-coders like doing. We wouldn't have started learning to code if we didn't enjoy it! It can be frustrating at times, but that makes it all the more satisfying when I finally figure a problem out.
